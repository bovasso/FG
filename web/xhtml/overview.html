Us Magazine

Highlights:

  • 2010 Webby Honoree
  • 2010 Communicator Award Silver / Award of Distinction

Client:

Wenner Media

Project Description:

Celebrity news, much like ice cream, has got to be a fresh scoop. That's why Wenner Media approached Funny Garbage to guide their Us Weekly print brand into an innovative internet experience that was streamlined for their editors and, in turn, instantaneous for their audience. Through extensive user testing, focus groups, and explorative Information Architecture, we introduced UsMagazine.com to a broad, hip, new audience of culture vultures, while emulating its magazine mother.

Funny Garbage reconceptualized the brand's look for the net and divided its magazine features into specific sections that would promote the most content to online users and best appeal to advertisers. Detailed categories like "Moms & Babies" and "Healthy Lifestyle" emerged to target precise demographics of the readership and sponsorship. Funny Garbage also designed a digital fram
ework that translated their iconic print columns with visual candy like image carousels and maximized their clickable potential as a web destination. Then, we cleverly sculpted the page layout to serve up the editorial work in blog format, while providing the ideal real estate for advertising and skins.

Behind the scenes, we took the site off its Drupal platform and built an even more customized Content Management System that simplified the workflow and collaboration between the editorial staff, photo editors, and technical support team. We listened to the blog personnel's needs and built a back end infrastructure that helped them all move together faster than the speed of gossip!

After a successful launch and partnership in 2009, Wenner Media asked Funny Garbage to stay with UsMagazine.com to provide the ongoing creative and technical services that continue to grow their internet business.
